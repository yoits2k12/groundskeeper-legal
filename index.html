Groundskeeper — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Groundskeeper ("the app," "we," "us") is a personal lawn and yard care tracking app. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices. We keep this simple: we only collect what's needed to run the app's features, and we do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Information You Provide

  • Account information: your email address and password, used to create and secure your account.
  • Yard details: information you enter about your yards — names, street address or location, ZIP code, grass type, size, sun exposure, and notes.
  • Activity you log: events such as mowing, watering, and fertilizing, including dates, notes, amounts, and any photos you add.
  • Products: product names and barcodes you choose to save.
  • Feedback: any messages you submit through the in-app feedback form.

Information From Your Device (with permission)

  • Camera & photo library: only used when you choose to take or attach a yard/event photo, or scan a product barcode. Barcode scans are processed on your device.
  • Location: if you allow it, your device location is used briefly to center the map when you draw a yard boundary. Your ZIP code (which you enter) is used to retrieve local weather. We do not continuously track your location.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the app's core features: tracking yards, logging activities, reminders, weather, and product history.
  • To show you local weather and rainfall for the ZIP codes you add.
  • To respond to feedback you send us.

Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the app. Information is shared with them only to provide functionality, not for advertising:

  • Supabase — stores your account, app data, and photos (database, authentication, and file storage).
  • Open-Meteo and OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — your ZIP code is sent to retrieve weather and to look up its approximate coordinates.
  • Google Maps — used to display the satellite map when you draw a yard boundary.
  • Google Firebase — included for push notifications (this feature is currently inactive in the app).

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We do not show advertising.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Data Security & Retention

Your data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections and stored with our service provider, Supabase. We retain your information for as long as your account exists.

Deleting Your Data

You may delete individual yards, events, products, and reminders in the app at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below and we will remove it.

Children

Groundskeeper is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
